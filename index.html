Utopian Thoughts
in the Bible

An interdisciplinary course exploring utopian thought in Scripture and its connections to theology, history, education, and civilization.

Taught by Dr. Mmeskerem Lechisa Debele

View the Course Register / Enroll
What you will learn

Explore utopian imagination
across history & scripture

01

Origins of Utopian Writing

Trace the history of "blueprint societies" and ideal-world thinking from ancient texts through the Renaissance.

02

Landmark Works

Study Thomas More's Utopia (1516) and Campanella's City of the Sun (1603) alongside biblical visions.

03

Identity & Civilization

Examine utopianism through the lens of Ethiopian history, culture, and intellectual tradition.

04

Modern Challenges

Connect ancient ideals to contemporary cultural, political, and educational questions of our time.
